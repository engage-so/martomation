Automated text messages are pre-written SMS messages that are automatically sent to customers based on certain criteria. Such automation criteria can involve a schedule (sent 2 hours before the start of a big sale), a timeline (sent one month after a customerâ€™s last purchase), or a trigger (sent immediately after a customer completes a purchase).

In this way, text message automations enable businesses to develop better connections with their audiences in a quick, cost-effective way.
